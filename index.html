How it works

From data to decisions in minutes

1

Connect your shop

Authorize ShopPulse via Etsy's official OAuth. We read your listings, stats, and shop data — nothing is scraped or stored without your permission.

2

Get your diagnosis

We analyze every listing against your competitive niche. Visibility gaps, conversion issues, pricing mismatches, content quality — each flagged with specific evidence.

3

Follow your action plan

Each week, you get 3 prioritized actions ranked by estimated impact. Not "improve your SEO" — specific changes to specific listings with clear reasoning.

4

Track what works

ShopPulse monitors what happens after you act. Over time, recommendations get smarter based on what actually moves the needle for your shop.

What makes this different

Not another research dashboard

What we don't do

  • Dump raw keyword data for you to interpret
  • Show estimated sales numbers with unknown accuracy
  • Grade your listings without saying what to fix first
  • Scrape Etsy pages or violate platform terms
  • Pretend we know things we don't

What we do instead

  • Rank your fixes by impact, not alphabetical order
  • Show reasoning and evidence for every recommendation
  • Track whether changes actually helped or hurt
  • Learn what works for your specific shop over time
  • Say "we don't know" when we don't know
