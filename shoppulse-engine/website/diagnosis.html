Real example

From a real diagnosis we ran on a custom portrait shop:

Finding #1 — Severity 7/10
6 of 8 listings show inflated original prices
"Custom Original Oil Painting" listed at $111.25 with crossed-out $430.87. Experienced Etsy buyers recognize this pattern — it signals a scam or low-quality seller.
Finding #2 — Severity 6/10
4 of 8 listings look nearly interchangeable
All at the same price of $111.25. Etsy deprioritizes shops with duplicate-looking listings, and buyers who see the same product repeated assume the shop lacks variety.
Finding #3 — Severity 5/10
All 8 listings permanently on sale
When everything is always on sale, nothing is actually on sale. This undermines trust — especially for a shop with 1 review.
