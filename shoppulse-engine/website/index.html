How it works

From data to decisions in minutes

1

Know your real margins

Use our free fee calculator to see what Etsy actually takes. Most sellers underestimate their fees by 30-40%.

2

Get your diagnosis

We analyze every listing against your competitive niche. Visibility, conversion, pricing, trust, content — each checked with specific evidence.

3

Follow your action plan

Each week, 3 prioritized actions ranked by impact. Not "improve your SEO" — specific changes to specific listings with clear reasoning.

4

Track what works

ShopPulse monitors what happens after you act. If a fix didn't work, we escalate to the next layer instead of repeating the same advice.

What makes this different

Not another research dashboard

What we don't do

  • Dump raw keyword data for you to interpret
  • Show estimated sales numbers with unknown accuracy
  • Grade your listings without saying what to fix first
  • Scrape Etsy pages or violate platform terms
  • Pretend we know things we don't

What we do instead

  • Rank your fixes by impact, not alphabetical order
  • Show reasoning and evidence for every recommendation
  • Track whether changes actually helped or hurt
  • Learn what works for your specific shop over time
  • Say "we don't know" when we don't know
