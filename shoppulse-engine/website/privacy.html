Privacy Policy

Effective: April 21, 2026

ShopPulse ("we," "us") provides a weekly action-planning tool for Etsy sellers. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights over it.

What we collect

When you connect your Etsy shop, ShopPulse accesses the following through Etsy's official Open API v3 via OAuth 2.0:

Your shop profile (shop name, description, policies); your active and expired listings (titles, tags, descriptions, prices, photos, views where available); your receipts and order history (for the purpose of calculating shop-level metrics); your shop reviews and ratings.

We do not access or store your Etsy password. We do not access buyer personal information beyond what is required to display order metrics to you.

How we use it

We use your Etsy data solely to:

Generate your weekly action recommendations; measure whether completed actions correlate with metric changes in your shop; display your own shop data back to you in the ShopPulse dashboard.

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. We do not use your Etsy data to train machine learning models. ShopPulse's scoring logic is deterministic rule-based Python code, not ML.

How we store it

Your data is stored in an encrypted database hosted on a reputable cloud provider. Access is limited to the ShopPulse application and its maintainers. We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

Your rights

You can disconnect ShopPulse from your Etsy shop at any time through your Etsy account settings under Shop Manager > Apps > Connected apps. You can request full deletion of your data by emailing us at the address below — we will delete it within 30 days.

Third parties

ShopPulse uses the following third-party services: Etsy (for shop data via OAuth), and our cloud hosting provider (for storage). We do not use advertising networks, analytics trackers, or social media pixels.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change this policy, we will notify connected users by email before the change takes effect.

Contact

Questions? Email hello@myshoppulse.com.
